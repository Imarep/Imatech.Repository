RECEIVED ORDER CONFIRMATION (ROC) PROCESS

REMINDERS

  • ALWAYS mention PO ref in the subject or first line of the email query to NL or when sending RFEDS/RFOCS
  • ONLY process ORDER CONFIRMATION email. PROFORMA INVOICE / SUPPLIER OTHER EMAILS should NOT be processed, just move to Follow up to Supplier folder instead (See more)
  • More than 10 line items can declare as idle time of maximum 10mins. only in tracker with corresponding remarks (PO ref)
  • Make your query as simple and clear as possible.
  • ALWAYS Put a memo regarding ANY discrepancy or query.

    Special Instructions

    1. Guidelines in sending a query to Vendor(Applicable to ROC / POCC / 079 Process)

      2. REMINDER When sending email to vendor and their PO reference is not mentioned in the subject email. Please MENTION the customer's reference in the subject before sending the email.

    2. Guidelines in sending a query to NL (Applicable to ROC / POCC / 079 Process)

      3. REMINDER: When sending email to NL and our imatech reference(all process) is not mentioned in the subject email. Please MENTION it in the subject or in the body of the email.
      REMINDER: Below scenarios are meant to guide you to understand the logic in identifying the document which has discrepancy. Analyze the logic, not the pattern.
      It is not meant to supersede the existing instructions we have, for instance, checking the vendor card for additional information such as afhalen, charges, etc.

      In case you still haven’t figured it out, if PO and OC are not the same, as long as we followed the offer and other conditions (may it be from the offer or vendor card), it should be check OC unless the OC favors us,
      then it’s otherwise (check PO).

      1. Identify the document that has discrepancy
        2. >> let us know the problem document
      2. What is the discrepancy
        3. >> tell us what is the problem with the document
      3. Supporting details to explain why do you think it is a discrepancy
        4. >> let us know the investigation you’ve done to explain why do you think that the discrepancy is a problem

        For instance:
        (1)Kindly check PO. (2)No discount was applied (3) while it is stated in the vendor card and confirmed in the OC.
        How to identify which document should be asked to be checked when processing OCs.

      1. QUANTITY
          • scenario 1:

          OC and PO: not equal
          -- check OC (regardless which one is lower or higher)
      2. DISCOUNT
          • scenario 1:

          offer and PO: same (or not, but offer is lower than OC)
          OC: higher
          -- check PO

          • scenario 2:

          offer and PO: same
          OC: lower
          -- check OC

          • scenario 3:

          offer and OC: same
          PO: other amount (regardless if lower or higher)
          -- check PO
      3. UNIT PRICE
          4. REMINDER: See instruction "Total Amount Accepted Difference" for reference.

          • scenario 1:

          offer and PO: same
          OC: higher
          -- check OC

          • scenario 2:

          offer and PO: same
          OC: lower
          -- check PO

          • scenario 3:

          offer and OC: same
          PO: other amount (regardless if lower or higher)
          -- check PO

          • scenario 4:

          offer and OC: same
          PO: other amount (regardless if lower or higher)
          -- check PO
      4. FREIGHT CHARGE
          • scenario 1:

          OC: none
          PO: with FC (regardless if it’s in the offer)
          -- check PO

          • scenario 2:

          OC: with FC
          offer and PO: none
          -- check OC

          • scenario 3:

          offer and OC: with FC
          PO: none
          -- check PO (please also mention if OC is higher)

          • scenario 4:

          offer and PO: same
          OC: higher
          -- check OC

          • scenario 5:

          offer and PO: same
          OC: lower
          -- check PO

    3. Total Amount Accepted Difference

      4. When processing an order conformation and total amount does not match (please make sure qty does match), there is NO need to inform NL when the difference is small.

      Please use the following :

      When total amount of PO is under 100 Euro, then accepted difference is until 2,50 Euro
      When total amount of PO is above 100 Euro, then accepted difference is until 5 Euro.

        • If NOT afhalen", message will be:

        -- "before [ETD]"

      Is it possible to deliver our order soonest?
      We are already late with delivery to our customer.
